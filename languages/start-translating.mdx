## Start translating

Quark uses Weblate to manage translations. This means that you can easily contribute to the translations of Quark by visiting our [Weblate project](https://languages.quark.bot/engage/quark-bot/).

## How to contribute

### 1. Sign up for a Weblate account [here](https://languages.quark.bot/accounts/register/).
Sign up for an account, and verify your email address. Your email will be public. If you don't want this, you can change it to the provided no-reply email in the next step.

### 2. Change your email to the provided no-reply email.
Head to your [profile settings](https://languages.quark.bot/accounts/profile/), and change your email to the provided no-reply email - it should look something like `username@users.noreply.languages.quark.bot`.

### 3. Start translating!
Visit the [Quark project](https://languages.quark.bot/engage/quark-bot/) on Weblate and start translating!
If a language you want to translate is not yet added, please contact us on [Discord](https://quark.bot/support).
