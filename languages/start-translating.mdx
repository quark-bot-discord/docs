## Start translating

Quark uses Weblate to manage translations. This means that you can easily contribute to the translations of Quark by visiting our [Weblate project](https://languages.quark.bot/engage/quark-bot/).

## How to contribute

<Steps titleSize="h3">
    <Step title="Sign up for a Weblate account.">
        Sign up for an account [here](https://languages.quark.bot/accounts/register/), and verify your email address. 
        <Warning>Your email will be public. If you don't want this, you can change it to the provided no-reply email in the next step.</Warning>
    </Step>
    <Step title="Change your email to the provided no-reply email.">
        Head to your [profile settings](https://languages.quark.bot/accounts/profile/), and change your email to the provided no-reply email - it should look something like `username@users.noreply.languages.quark.bot`.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Start translating!">
        Visit the [Quark project](https://languages.quark.bot/engage/quark-bot/) on Weblate and start translating!
        <Tip>If a language you want to translate is not yet added, take a look at our [Adding a new language page](/languages/add-language).</Tip>
    </Step>
</Steps>
